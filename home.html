What is The Coding Sessions

The Coding Sessions was created with a desire to inspire women to feel comfortable and confident to get their nerd on, and explore the exciting world of Tech! We offer an online educational hub with courses and resources that encourage women to feel confident in taking their first steps into Web Development.

What We Do

We are here to deliver support, training, and education to women who want to learn how to code. We do this through our premium 1:1 training services and courses, specifically designed to empower the minds of women who feel that learning code is a puzzle.

Learning how to code can become overwhelming. More often than not, not knowing where to start, or feeling a lack of support can often lead women to feel uncertain about their abilities, or feel like they aren't "equipped" with enough knowledge or confidence to want to continue with their persuit of their learning.
