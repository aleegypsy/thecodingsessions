The Coding Sessions was created with a desire to inspire women to get their nerd on, and learn the art of coding. Our online educational hub offers courses and resources that encourage women to feel confident in taking their first steps into Web Development.

Our passion to help fill a void that is currently left by women in the tech industry (80% of women), has fuelled a need for us to build a strong and elevating space that will empower and excite women to get their kickstart into the world of coding.

Our mission is simple: empower women to learn the art of coding.

The Art of Coding

Let's not get technical here with this whole art of coding business, because who's got time for that?! Our focus is to teach women to consider creativity, thought, intuition, and self-expression in the way you structure code. That is what we would consider artistry in itself!

But, why Code?

Steve Jobs once said, "Everybody should learn how to program a computer... because it teaches you how to think." And as biased as this may sound, we have to agree with him! You see, coding helps develop structured and creative thinking. It forces you to break down problems bit by bit, to make sense of them, and to find the logic before solving the problem.

Doesn't sound sexy enough? Well, learning to code also teaches you persistence. When you learn the art of coding, your brain starts functioning with seeing problems as solutions, and you start to forsee the possible ways to solve it. You develop determination to act on solving the problem, and you start thinking logically about a problem.

Code vs The Real World

As you can see, developing these skills can translate into solving difficult situations in your everyday life. You become patient because you know there is always a solution. You become determined and persistent when things aren't going the way you envisioned. You develop confidence, because you can face anything that gets thrown your way.

The amazing thing is, you only need to know the basics of coding to imagine all the solutions you can work to solve. Imagine the things you can create?!

We are here to guide you on your first steps into Web Development.
