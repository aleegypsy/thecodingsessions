Four P's of Coding

Passion

You need to want to learn. It's as simple as that. People who have passion, will want to keep learning.

Patience

Endure the challenges. Believe us, there will be plenty. But patience is a virtue in the art of coding.

Persistence

Stick it out. The more you practice, the faster you will learn and the more efficient you become.

Perseverance

Don't give up! Whatever you do, keep at it and keep challenging yourself.
